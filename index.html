Habito is an app that helps you keep track of your habits. You can mark down your progress daily and at the end of each week you will be prompted to write down how your week successes and struggles.

The end goal is to reach 5000 points. Every sucessful day grants you a combo point and +10 score points, the more combo points you have, the more points you will receive. Inversely if you fail a day you will lose your combo points and start from 10 points again.

There is a substantial bonus at the end of each week. You will receive a 50% bonus from every point you've gathered/lost during that certain week. Additionally at the end of each month you will get a 100% bonus from all the points you've gathered/lost during said month.

Ranks:
0-500 = Wood
500-1500 = Silver
1500-3000 = Platinum
3000-5000 = Diamond
>5000 = Master
